Scenario Overview

This challenge tasks you with investigating a cyber attack on Company FlaireFarm and the disappearance of the perpetrator. Your objective is to collect information from various online sources to uncover the hacker's identity and location.

Your Mission

Company FlaireFarm, a leading tech firm, was recently hit by a cyber attack resulting in significant data breaches and financial losses. The perpetrator, a skilled hacker, has vanished, leaving behind digital clues.

Get Started

You're allowed to request two hints during this challenge at any point and submit answers on the provided Google Docs.

  1. What is the alias used by the attacker?
  2. What is the real name and surname of the attacker?
  3. What's the username used by the attacker on social media?
  4. Which University did the attacker attend and in what years?
  5. What's the email address of the attacker?
  6. List the places visited by the attacker.
  7. What operating system does the attacker use?
  8. What is the other username which you've discovered along with operating system?
  9. Which company is the next target of the attacker, submit random string which you've found
