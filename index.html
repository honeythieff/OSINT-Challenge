Scenario Overview

This challenge tasks you with investigating a cyber attack on Company FlaireFarm and the disappearance of the perpetrator. Your objective is to collect information from various online sources to uncover the hacker's identity and location.

Your Mission

Company FlaireFarm, a leading tech firm, was recently hit by a cyber attack resulting in significant data breaches and financial losses. The perpetrator, a skilled hacker, has vanished, leaving behind digital clues.

Get Started

Your task is to employ Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) techniques to gather information about the hacker. This includes analyzing social media profiles, conducting reverse image searches, and searching for public records or news articles related to the attack. Begin your investigation using the tools and resources provided during the lecture. Good luck!
