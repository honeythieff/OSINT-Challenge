Scenario Overview

This challenge tasks you with investigating a cyber attack on Company FlaireFarm and the disappearance of the perpetrator. Your objective is to collect information from various online sources to uncover the hacker's identity and location.

Your Mission

Company FlaireFarm, a leading tech firm, was recently hit by a cyber attack resulting in significant data breaches and financial losses. The perpetrator, a skilled hacker, has vanished, leaving behind digital clues.

Get Started

You're allowed to request two hints during this challenge at any point, submit answers on the provided GoogleDocs During this Challenge you have to answer following questions: 1. What is the alias used by attacker? 2. What is the real name and surname of the attacker? 3. Whats the username used by attacker on social media? 4. Which University did attacker attend and what years? 5. What's the email address of the attacker? 6. List the places visited by the attacker 7. What operating system does user use? 8. Which company is the next target of the attacker?
